HYDRAULIC MEASUREMENT AND CONTROL

We are specialist manufacturers of hydraulic measurement and control products helping to improve the productivity of heavy machinery.

For 60 years we have been helping customers worldwide in the industrial, mobile, and agricultural sectors to diagnose hydraulic faults, verify hydraulic conditions and achieve repeatable hydraulic control.

Using our Make it BLUE® manufacturing process we help customers maximise the potential of their hydraulic machinery but without the complexity of costly consultancy.
